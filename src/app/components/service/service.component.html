Citizen Connect Services

Real-Time Issue Reporting

Citizens can report and track community issues such as potholes, broken streetlights, and other infrastructure problems.

Petition System

Users can create and sign petitions for community-driven initiatives, enabling citizens to advocate for positive change.

Interactive Voting

Citizens can participate in voting on proposed projects and policies, ensuring inclusive decision-making.

Virtual Town Halls

Attend government meetings through live streaming, fostering transparency and real-time engagement with policymakers.
